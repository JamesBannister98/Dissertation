About

Davis is a chatbot created for my dissertation to support you with your daily wellbeing. He can help offer to give you some help and guidance for some coping mechanisms on any stress, anxiety and depression you may be feeling. He is not equipped to give advice or aid to any conditions besides this one and if it is an emergency situation please contact the relevant services instead. Their contact information can be found in the contacts link at the top of this page.

Davis has been created using some basic HTML and CSS language to create this site and his knowledge base and programming comes from the Microsoft bot framework. Feel free to give him a try and see if he can help you reduce your stress in your day to day life.
